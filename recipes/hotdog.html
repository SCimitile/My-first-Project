Hotdogs

Description

A Hotdog is a simple, yet delicious food. Whenever you are hungry and you do not know what to eat, the hotdog is always a suitable solution.

And the best part is that you just need 3 ingredientes. And kids loves them.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Sausage
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Cook the sausages antil they float aver the water
  2. Cut the bread in half
  3. Put the sausage in the brad
  4. Add some mustard
  5. Enjoy
