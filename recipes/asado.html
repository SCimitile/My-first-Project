Asado

Description

The photo above is the very best example of what an argentinian asado looks like. The "Asado argentino" (as the natives refer to it) is not just meat. It is not plainly roasted by a fire, but by the embers, getting that distintic smoked taste that makes it unique.

in this page we are going to learn how to cook the meat properly in order to turn it into a real "Asado"

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Salt
  • "Chimichurri" (a sot of sauce made of spices and vinager)
  • Any kind of salad
  • Bread
  • Wood to burn

Steps

  1. Light up a fire until the embers are "pale".
  2. Clean the "parrilla"(the grill) with fire.
  3. Put the meat by de embers over the grill while there is no to much heat so the meat will not be raw on the inside and burnt on the outside.
  4. Meanwhile it cooks prepare the salat.
  5. When ready turn over the meat
  6. When the meat is ready you can cut it and serve it.
  7. Enjoy!
